Unreal Realistic World

Personal projet created on Unreal Engine 4.
I created a realistic world in real time responding to the seasons, time of the day and weather. I developed and used shaders and blueprints on Unreal Engine 4.

Lullaby For a Fish

Lullaby for a Fish is a game developped for my final year with a team of 5 people. It is a relaxing game where the player has to solve puzzles to make progress in the story. He uses the sound of his pan flute to interact with objects and find clues in a lowpoly environment. The story is inspired by the tale "the golden fish" where an old fisherman calls a magic fish to grant his wife's wishes.
The game is developed on Unity 3D, we create the 3D assets on Blender. We develop in C# all the interactions of the sound and the gameplay.

Renard'O


Immersive application realized in augmented reality on Unity (with Vuforia) during my internship at la Méduse Violette. The application is based on the story of Renard'O, a water-coated fox, who is walking around the Sainte Roseline wine estate during the Green Vendange event.
As project manager, I had to conceive the different activities and think about the different issues of game design. I also developed the application under Unity and designed Augmented Reality interactions with Vuforia (developed C# scripts), in order to make the different animations in the 3D scene interact with the player.

Explor'a la Défense


Realised in the second year of IMAC in collaboration with the company L'équipe Ludique, Explor'à la Défense is a team building treasure hunt taking place on the La Défense site.

"Dive into a futuristic universe and travel through time. After accidentally landing in 2020, repair and find your equipment before it's too late. Thanks to your team spirit, try to go back in time while discovering the different monuments of La Défense. »

This is a 2D application developed under Unity, for a treasure hunt on the La Défense site. The team building team has to solve the different puzzles of the application by observing the surrounding area but also by searching in a backpack at their disposal. We designed the scenario and the different puzzles of the game. We also developed the application under Unity, and coded the different puzzles in C#.

Project IMACWORLD


This project consists in the realisation of a 3D terrain and scene editor and viewer. A certain number of functionalities are implemented in order to allow the user to create a world from cubic blocks and to navigate within it. It addresses key notions of C++ programming, 3D rendering (OpenGL) and numerical interpolation .
